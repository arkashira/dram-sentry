### Phase‑by‑Phase Deep Dive

#### 1. Aware
- **Trigger**: Hardware teams hear about DDR5 price volatility at a trade show or read an industry blog.
- **Friction**: The niche nature of DRAM price monitoring means few people know about dram‑sentry.
- **Emotion**: Curiosity mixed with anxiety over rising component costs.
- **Delight**: A short, data‑rich explainer video that instantly shows the magnitude of price swings and how dram‑sentry can help.
- **Metric**: Aim for 12 k monthly website visits and a 5 % ad CTR.

#### 2. Consider
- **Trigger**: Downloading a free market snapshot or requesting a demo.
- **Friction**: Demo scheduling delays and lack of clear ROI data.
- **Emotion**: Skepticism, but hopeful that a solution exists.
- **Delight**: Live demo with a use‑case tailored to the prospect’s procurement workflow, plus an ROI calculator that quantifies potential savings.
- **Metric**: 250 demo requests per month, 30 % of those becoming MQLs.

#### 3. Try
- **Trigger**: Activation of a 14‑day free trial.
- **Friction**: Slow data feeds and complex alert setup.
- **Emotion**: Excitement tempered by caution.
- **Delight**: One‑click alert wizard, real‑time AI predictions, and 24/7 chat support.
- **Metric**: 180 trial activations per month, 70 % of trial users adopting core features.

#### 4. Adopt
- **Trigger**: First price‑spike alert leads to a cost‑saving decision.
- **Friction**: Pricing concerns for small teams, limited integration depth.
- **Emotion**: Confidence, satisfaction.
- **Delight**: Affordable Starter tier, quarterly cost‑review sessions, and a best‑practice guide.
- **Metric**: 65 % of trial users convert to paid, churn < 3 %.

#### 5. Expand
- **Trigger**: Company growth (new data centers, larger procurement budgets).
- **Friction**: Scaling alert volume, need for advanced analytics.
- **Emotion**: Loyalty, proactive engagement.
- **Delight**: Enterprise tier, dedicated account manager, referral program.
- **Metric**: 25 % upsell rate, NPS of 75.
